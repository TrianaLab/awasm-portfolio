# Code of Conduct

This project adopts the [Contributor Covenant v2.0](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/0/code_of_conduct/) as its Code of Conduct.

We are committed to providing a welcoming and inclusive environment for everyone. Participants are expected to act respectfully toward other contributors, maintainers, and users.

## Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, please report it by contacting the maintainer [@edu-diaz](https://github.com/edu-diaz). All reports will be reviewed promptly and treated as confidential.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may take any action they deem appropriate in response to violations, up to and including a permanent ban from project spaces.

Full text and enforcement guidelines: https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/0/code_of_conduct/
